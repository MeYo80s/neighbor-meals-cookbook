# Baked Ziti

**Recipe Owner:** Neighbor Meals
**Serves/Yield:** 8
**Prep time:** 20 min
**Cook time:** 40 min
**Estimated macros (auto):** ~456 cal | 25g protein | 16g fat | 52g carbs (per serving, serves 8)

### Ingredients
- 1 lb ziti pasta
- 1 jar marinara sauce
- 15 oz ricotta cheese
- 2 cups mozzarella, shredded
- 1/2 cup parmesan
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning

### Instructions
1. Cook pasta until just al dente.
2. Mix ricotta, egg, and seasoning.
3. Layer pasta, sauce, ricotta mixture, and cheeses in foil pan.
4. Cover and freeze.

### To Serve
1. Thaw overnight.
2. Bake at 375F for 35 to 40 minutes.

### Transport Notes
- Foil pan with lid works best for delivery and reheating.

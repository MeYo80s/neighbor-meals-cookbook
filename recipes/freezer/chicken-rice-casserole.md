# Chicken and Rice Casserole

**Recipe Owner:** Neighbor Meals
**Serves/Yield:** 6
**Prep time:** 15 min
**Cook time:** 40 min
**Estimated macros (auto):** ~390 cal | 31g protein | 18g fat | 24g carbs (per serving, serves 6)

### Ingredients
- 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

### Instructions
1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
2. Spread into a greased 9x13-inch foil pan.
3. Cover tightly with foil and freeze.

### To Serve
1. Thaw overnight in fridge.
2. Bake at 350F for 35 to 40 minutes, until hot and bubbly.

### Transport Notes
- Deliver in a disposable foil pan with a clear reheat label.

# Turkey Chili

**Recipe Owner:** Neighbor Meals
**Serves/Yield:** 8
**Prep time:** 15 min
**Cook time:** 30 min
**Estimated macros (auto):** ~250 cal | 22g protein | 6g fat | 29g carbs (per serving, serves 8)

### Ingredients
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cans diced tomatoes
- 2 cans kidney beans, drained
- 1 can black beans, drained
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp salt

### Instructions
1. Brown turkey with onion and garlic.
2. Stir in remaining ingredients.
3. Simmer 25 to 30 minutes.
4. Cool fully, portion into freezer bags, and freeze flat.

### To Serve
1. Thaw overnight.
2. Reheat on stovetop or in microwave until hot.

### Transport Notes
- Use a leak-proof container and pack cornbread separately.

# Neighbor Meals Recipe Book

A collection of simple, comforting meals designed for sharing with neighbors and friends during times of need - illness, loss, recovery, or welcoming a new baby. Includes home-cooked recipes, freezer meals prepared in advance, and thoughtful ready-to-purchase drop-off options.

---

## Freezer Meals (Prep Ahead)

### 1. Chicken & Rice Casserole
**Serves:** 6  
**Freezer-friendly:** Up to 3 months

**Ingredients**
- 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
- 2 cups cooked rice
- 1 can cream of chicken soup
- 1 cup sour cream
- 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
- 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
- 1/2 tsp garlic powder
- Salt and pepper to taste

**Directions**
1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
2. Spread into a greased 9x13-inch foil pan.
3. Cover tightly with foil and freeze.

**To serve**
1. Thaw overnight in fridge.
2. Bake at 350F for 35-40 minutes, until hot and bubbly.

---

### 2. Turkey Chili
**Serves:** 6-8  
**Freezer-friendly:** Up to 3 months

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb ground turkey
- 1 onion, diced
- 2 cloves garlic, minced
- 2 cans diced tomatoes
- 2 cans kidney beans, drained
- 1 can black beans, drained
- 2 tbsp chili powder
- 1 tsp cumin
- 1 tsp salt

**Directions**
1. Brown turkey with onion and garlic.
2. Stir in remaining ingredients.
3. Simmer 25-30 minutes.
4. Cool fully, portion into freezer bags, and freeze flat.

**To serve**
1. Thaw overnight, then reheat on stovetop or in microwave.

---

### 3. Baked Ziti
**Serves:** 8  
**Freezer-friendly:** Up to 3 months

**Ingredients**
- 1 lb ziti pasta
- 1 jar marinara sauce
- 15 oz ricotta cheese
- 2 cups mozzarella, shredded
- 1/2 cup parmesan
- 1 egg
- 1 tsp Italian seasoning

**Directions**
1. Cook pasta until just al dente.
2. Mix ricotta, egg, and seasoning.
3. Layer pasta, sauce, ricotta mixture, and cheeses in foil pan.
4. Cover and freeze.

**To serve**
1. Thaw overnight.
2. Bake at 375F for 35-40 minutes.

## Homemade Meals That Travel Well (Simple)

### 1. Baked Pasta
**Why it works:** One-pan meal, easy to reheat.  
**Transport tip:** Use a foil pan with a tight lid.

### 2. Chicken & Rice Bowls
**Why it works:** Balanced and mild, easy for most families.  
**Transport tip:** Pack in individual containers for quick serving.

### 3. Beef or Turkey Chili
**Why it works:** Simple ingredients and freezes well.  
**Transport tip:** Deliver in a leak-proof container with cornbread on the side.

### 4. Soup + Rolls
**Why it works:** Comforting and gentle when someone is under the weather.  
**Transport tip:** Use a sealed soup container; pack rolls separately.

### 5. Breakfast Casserole
**Why it works:** Helpful for new parents and easy morning meals.  
**Transport tip:** Bake in a disposable pan and include reheating directions.

---

## Helpful Delivery Notes

- Label each meal with reheating instructions.
- Include allergen notes (dairy, gluten, nuts).
- Use disposable pans when possible to avoid return logistics.
- Add a simple note of encouragement.
- If appropriate, bring a complete meal: main dish + side + simple dessert.

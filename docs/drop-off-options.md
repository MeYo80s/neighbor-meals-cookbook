# Ready-to-Purchase Drop-Off Options

These are excellent when time is limited but support is still needed.

## Rotisserie Chicken Dinner Kit
- 1 rotisserie chicken
- Bagged salad and dressing
- Bakery rolls
- Pre-cut fruit

## Soup and Bread Bundle
- 2 large deli soups (ready-to-heat)
- Fresh bakery bread
- Butter or olive oil dip

## Breakfast Care Package
- Dozen bagels or muffins
- Cream cheese or jam
- Fresh fruit
- Juice or herbal tea

## Comfort Snack Basket
- Crackers
- Cheese slices
- Nuts
- Dried fruit
- Granola bars

## Child-Friendly Night
- Store-bought mac and cheese tray
- Steamed veggie tray
- Applesauce pouches
- Frozen cookies to bake

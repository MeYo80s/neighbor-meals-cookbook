1) Chicken and Rice Casserole

Recipe Owner: Neighbor Meals

Serves/Yield: 6

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 40 min

Estimated macros (auto): ~390 cal | 31g protein | 18g fat | 24g carbs (per serving, serves 6)

Ingredients

  • 3 cups cooked chicken, shredded
  • 2 cups cooked rice
  • 1 can cream of chicken soup
  • 1 cup sour cream
  • 1 cup shredded cheddar cheese
  • 1 cup frozen mixed vegetables
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Mix all ingredients in a large bowl.
  2. Spread into a greased 9x13-inch foil pan.
  3. Cover tightly with foil and freeze.

To Serve

  1. Thaw overnight in fridge.
  2. Bake at 350F for 35 to 40 minutes, until hot and bubbly.

Transport Notes

  • Deliver in a disposable foil pan with a clear reheat label.

2) Turkey Chili

Recipe Owner: Neighbor Meals

Serves/Yield: 8

Prep time: 15 min

Cook time: 30 min

Estimated macros (auto): ~250 cal | 22g protein | 6g fat | 29g carbs (per serving, serves 8)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground turkey
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans kidney beans, drained
  • 1 can black beans, drained
  • 2 tbsp chili powder
  • 1 tsp cumin
  • 1 tsp salt

Instructions

  1. Brown turkey with onion and garlic.
  2. Stir in remaining ingredients.
  3. Simmer 25 to 30 minutes.
  4. Cool fully, portion into freezer bags, and freeze flat.

To Serve

  1. Thaw overnight.
  2. Reheat on stovetop or in microwave until hot.

Transport Notes

  • Use a leak-proof container and pack cornbread separately.

3) Baked Ziti

Recipe Owner: Neighbor Meals

Serves/Yield: 8

Prep time: 20 min

Cook time: 40 min

Estimated macros (auto): ~456 cal | 25g protein | 16g fat | 52g carbs (per serving, serves 8)

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ziti pasta
  • 1 jar marinara sauce
  • 15 oz ricotta cheese
  • 2 cups mozzarella, shredded
  • 1/2 cup parmesan
  • 1 egg
  • 1 tsp Italian seasoning

Instructions

  1. Cook pasta until just al dente.
  2. Mix ricotta, egg, and seasoning.
  3. Layer pasta, sauce, ricotta mixture, and cheeses in foil pan.
  4. Cover and freeze.

To Serve

  1. Thaw overnight.
  2. Bake at 375F for 35 to 40 minutes.

Transport Notes

  • Foil pan with lid works best for delivery and reheating.

Homemade Meals That Travel Well (Simple)

Baked Pasta

  • Why it works: One-pan meal, easy to reheat.
  • Transport tip: Use a foil pan with a tight lid.

Chicken and Rice Bowls

  • Why it works: Balanced and mild, easy for most families.
  • Transport tip: Pack in individual containers for quick serving.

Beef or Turkey Chili

  • Why it works: Simple ingredients and freezes well.
  • Transport tip: Deliver in a leak-proof container with cornbread on the side.

Soup and Rolls

  • Why it works: Comforting and gentle when someone is under the weather.
  • Transport tip: Use a sealed soup container; pack rolls separately.

Breakfast Casserole

  • Why it works: Helpful for new parents and easy morning meals.
  • Transport tip: Bake in a disposable pan and include reheating directions.

Helpful Delivery Notes

  • Label each meal with reheating instructions.
  • Include allergen notes (dairy, gluten, nuts).
  • Use disposable pans when possible to avoid return logistics.
  • Add a simple note of encouragement.
  • If appropriate, bring a complete meal: main dish, side, and simple dessert.
